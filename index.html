Projects

Featured project

Concept exploration

Bridge the Gap

Bridge the Gap is a concept for a cross-cultural platform that uses food as a medium for connection and cultural understanding. The idea emerged from encountering unfamiliar ingredients in international grocery stores—where curiosity exists but knowledge about how ingredients are used culturally is often missing.

The project explores how a digital platform could combine ingredient discovery, cultural storytelling, and community interaction to help people understand how food connects cultures. The long-term vision is to create a space where food becomes a bridge for cultural exchange among international students, migrants, and travelers.
