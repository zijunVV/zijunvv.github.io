I am a strategy-oriented analyst with 4+ years of experience translating complex data, stakeholder requirements, and ambiguous business problems into clear insights, recommendations, dashboards, reports, and practical digital solutions.

My industry work combines product management, data analysis, sustainability analytics, and cross-functional delivery. I am especially interested in consulting, sustainability, business analysis, and digital transformation roles where analytical thinking and stakeholder communication drive measurable impact.
