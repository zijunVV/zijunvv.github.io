About

I am a strategy-oriented analyst with a background in data, product, and computational social science, driven by a strong curiosity about how technology shapes human behavior and interaction.

Across both industry and research experiences, I have consistently explored how data can be used to understand human behavior and inform decision-making—from environmental modeling to digital product optimization. This journey has shaped my interest in data-driven strategy, especially in cross-cultural and technology-enabled contexts.

My professional experience spans digital product development and applied data science. I have worked on building and optimizing user-facing products through data analysis, experimentation, and cross-functional collaboration, while also engaging in research that applies machine learning to real-world environmental challenges. These experiences have equipped me with both analytical rigor and a practical understanding of how insights can be translated into impact.

Beyond my professional work, I am particularly interested in the role of culture in shaping behavior and decision-making. I am currently developing Bridge the Gap, a concept for a cross-cultural platform that uses food as a medium to foster understanding and connection between people from different backgrounds. This reflects my broader aspiration to combine data, technology, and human-centered thinking to create meaningful and inclusive solutions.

I am especially interested in opportunities at the intersection of data, strategy, and innovation—where I can contribute to solving complex problems, particularly in global and multicultural contexts.

Projects

Featured Project

Concept exploration

Bridge the Gap

Bridge the Gap is a concept for a cross-cultural platform that uses food as a medium for connection and cultural understanding. It explores how digital tools could combine ingredient discovery, cultural storytelling, and community interaction to reveal the cultural knowledge embedded in everyday food. By turning curiosity about unfamiliar ingredients into opportunities for learning and sharing, the project ultimately seeks to foster mutual understanding and more friendly exchanges among people from different cultures.
