Featured Project

Concept exploration

Bridge the Gap

Bridge the Gap is a concept for a cross-cultural platform that uses food as a medium for connection and cultural understanding. It explores how digital tools could combine ingredient discovery, cultural storytelling, and community interaction to reveal the cultural knowledge embedded in everyday food. By turning curiosity about unfamiliar ingredients into opportunities for learning and sharing, the project ultimately seeks to foster mutual understanding and more friendly exchanges among people from different cultures.
